Our Staff

We have a team of committed and qualified teaching and non-teaching staff who are ready to develop your child academically.
The primary section is managed by nine classroom teachers including a deputy headteacher and headteacher while the nursery section is handled by four early childhood development teachers
Our non-teaching staff includes a bursar, storekeeper, security guard, and two cooks. Each unit performs its functions meticulously to achieve the overall goal of the school
