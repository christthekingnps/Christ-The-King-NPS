Our Staff

We have a committed team of teaching and non-teaching staff dedicated to the academic and holistic development of your child.

The primary section is managed by nine classroom teachers, including a headteacher and deputy headteacher, while the nursery section is handled by four early childhood development teachers.

Non-teaching staff includes a bursar, storekeeper, security guard, and two cooks. Each unit diligently performs its role to ensure the smooth functioning of the school.
