Our Teaching Philosophy

Our teaching philosophy is guided by Christian values and a commitment to holistic child development.

The nursery section has been operational since February 2018, serving children under five years old. Pupils are enrolled from K-1 to K-3.

We provide a conducive learning environment that promotes academic excellence, physical development, and mental well-being.

Pupils are regularly assessed through structured classroom activities, mid-term evaluations, and end-of-term examinations.
