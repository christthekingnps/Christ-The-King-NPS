Best education for a bright future

Our teaching philosophy is guided by the Christ the saviour

The primary section has been operational since February 2018 targeting children above six years old. The current enrollment is 148 pupils spread from primary one to seven. The school is built on the foundation of promoting top quality education for a great nation of Uganda. The school has created a conducive learning environment for your child that includes both physical and metal fitness. Academic excellence is our priority. This is why we give our pupils adequate teaching and regularly assessed through tests, mid-term and end of term examinations
