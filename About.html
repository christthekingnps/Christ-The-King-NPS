Background

Christ the King Nursery and Primary School is a privately managed institution founded in 2017 and became fully operational in February 2018.

The idea for the school was first conceived when Mrs. Sylvia Omodo was working at Iceme Primary School, where she successfully started a nursery section. Inspired by that success, and before her retirement from government service, she began planning the establishment of a privately owned primary school.

She joined forces with her husband, Mr. Omodo Denis, to mobilize resources and begin operations. The school experienced challenges during the COVID-19 pandemic in 2020.

Sylvia passed away on 5 August 2021 after a long illness. Her family chose to preserve her legacy by continuing to manage the school and uphold her vision of expanding access to quality education for rural children.

She is remembered as an educationist who transformed education in Oyam District and beyond in her roles as a classroom teacher, headteacher, and Acting District Education Officer.

Our core values include honesty, respect, dignity, accountability, and transparency. We improve education quality through a holistic approach that ensures a conducive learning environment, strong staff recruitment and retention, and continuous capacity building.
