Christ the King Nursery and Primary School

Background

Christ the King NPS is a privately managed school that was founded in 2017 and became fully operational in February 2018.
The idea about the school was first conceived when Mrs. Sylvia Omodo (founder member) was working at Iceme Primary School where she
started a nursery section and that proved to be successful that before her retirement from government service, she started the plan
to establish a privately owned primary school. That was when she joined forces with her husband, Mr. Omodo Denis to mobilize resource
to start the operation of the school. Its operation was restricted during the covid-19 pandemic that began ravaging the world in 2020
Sylvia passed away on 5 August 2021 after a long illness with cancer and it was only natural that her family decided to preserve her legacy
by continuing to manage the school to realize her vision of promoting access to an excellent education among rural children.
Forever, she will be remembered as an educationist who transformed education in Oyam District and beyond in her capacity as a classroom teacher,
headteacher, and the acting District Education Officer over several decades of service to the nation.
Our core values include honesty, respect, diginity, accountability and transparency.
We are improving the quality of education within the region through an integrated approach that does not only entails having a conducive learning
environment for the pupils but also recruitment, retention and capacity building for our staff.
